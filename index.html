I am currently an assistant professor for the Department of Computer Science at Hanyang University.

Before joining Hanyang University, I was a post-doc researcher at Seoul National University.
I worked on designing a scalable computer architecture for brain-computer interfacing.

Before working as a post-doc researcher, I earned my Ph.D degree from Seoul National University under the supervision of Prof. Jangwoo Kim.
During my Ph.D., I focused on designing a brain simulation accelerator and analog-based process-in-memory architecture.
I also earned my BS degree from Seoul National University.

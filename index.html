I am a researcher at the Automation and Systems Research Institute (ASRI), advised by Prof. Jangwoo Kim.

During my Ph.D., I mostly focused on designing an optimal brain modeling architecture with flexible, efficient, and scalable brain simulation support (FlexBrain Project).

And I'm currently extending the project to design an architecture that cooperates with the biological system to realize true biological intelligence.

I'm also interested in other computer architecture topics including deep/machine learning accelerators, process-in-memory architectures, and CPU microarchitectures.
